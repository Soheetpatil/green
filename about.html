Who We Are

Shree Maruti Agrotech is a leading manufacturer of premium agricultural mulch films in India, dedicated to revolutionizing farming practices through innovative solutions. With over a decade of experience in the agricultural sector, we understand the unique challenges faced by Indian farmers.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, durable, and cost-effective mulch films that help farmers increase crop yield, conserve water, and reduce labor costs. We combine traditional farming wisdom with modern technology to create products that truly make a difference.

Based in the heart of India's agricultural belt, we are committed to sustainable farming practices and environmental conservation. Our biodegradable mulch films represent our dedication to eco-friendly solutions.
